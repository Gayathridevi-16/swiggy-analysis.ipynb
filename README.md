# 🍽️ Swiggy Data Analysis – Business Insights

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Swiggy food delivery data to extract meaningful business insights using Python. The goal is to understand customer behavior, sales trends, and revenue distribution to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Sales (Overall revenue generated)
- Average Rating (Customer satisfaction level)
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Total Orders
- Ratings Count

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Monthly, Weekly & Daily Sales Trends
- Veg vs Non-Veg Revenue Comparison
- State-wise & City-wise Sales Distribution
- Quarterly Performance Summary
- Top 5 Cities by Sales

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 📅 Weekend sales have higher order volumes compared to weekdays  
- 🌆 Top 5 cities contribute a major portion of total revenue  
- 🍗 Non-veg orders generate higher revenue than veg orders  
- 📈 Consistent growth observed in monthly sales trends
